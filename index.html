Why LogicLab

Built for learning by doing

LogicLab turns digital electronics theory into something you can click, drag and watch respond in real time — no lab kit, breadboard or backend required. Every simulator runs entirely in your browser and keeps working offline once loaded.

Under the hood

LogicLab is built from the ground up on a foundation of pure web technologies—HTML5, CSS3. It uses hand-coded simulators to keep computations fast. Best of all, it works as an installable PWA that functions flawlessly without an internet connection.

  • Semantic HTML5, hand-written CSS3, vanilla ES6 — no frameworks
  • Quine–McCluskey based K-Map minimizer
  • Custom event-driven circuit evaluation engine
  • Installable as a Progressive Web App
